📋 Admission Process — Simple & Clear

Follow these easy steps to secure admission for your child at Sanskriti Vidyapeeth

📝
Step 1

Fill Online Form

Visit our portal and fill the admission form with required details.

📄
Step 2

Submit Documents

Upload birth certificate, Aadhar, and previous school TC.

🧪
Step 3

Assessment Test

Appear for aptitude test (Class III+). Primary classes have interaction round.

👨‍👩‍👧
Step 4

Parent Interview

Brief interaction with the Principal and admission committee.

✅
Step 5

Confirmation

Pay admission fee and collect welcome kit. Welcome to Sanskriti!
